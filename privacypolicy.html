Example Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with the 13 principles of the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020:

Information We Collect:

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any other details you provide to us. This information is collected when you voluntarily submit it to us through our website's forms or interactions.

How We Use Your Information:

Your information is used for the purposes outlined at the time of collection, such as responding to your queries, improving user experience, or providing you with services. We do not share your information with third parties unless explicitly stated or required by law.

Your Rights:

You have the right to access any personal information we hold about you and to request its correction if it is inaccurate. You may also request that we delete your personal data or opt-out of data collection at any time.

Data Security:

We take reasonable steps to ensure that your personal information is securely stored and protected from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

Retention:

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

Cross-border Disclosure:

If we need to share your information with overseas entities, we will ensure that the information is handled securely and in accordance with New Zealand privacy laws.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, or if you would like to exercise your rights under the Privacy Act, please contact us.
